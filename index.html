Some info about SSAT and ISEE

  • 4 levels

    There are Primary, Lower, Middle and Upper Level in ISEE test

  • 1 Flex test per year

    You may only take one Flex test in a testing year.

  • 3 levels

    There are Elementary, Middle and Upper Level in SSAT test

  • 3 hours, 5 minutes

    This is how long the SSAT Middle and Upper Level test takes

  • 2 hours, 5minutes

    This is how long the SSAT Elementary Level test takes

  • 3 attempts

    Students may register to take the ISEE one time in any or all of three testing seasons.

  • 2 hours, 40 minutes

    This is how long the ISEE Middle and Upper Level test takes

  • 53 minutes

    This is how long the ISEE Primary Level test takes

Be engaged

Henrik Edberg`s simple ways to motivate yourself

  • 1

    Remember your successes. And let them flow through your mind instead of your failures. Write down your successes.

  • 2

    Make each day count. We don’t have all the time in the world. So focus on today and do the things you really want to do.

  • 3

    Think outside your box. Don’t imagine the future from the box of what you have now. Just because your mind is in box of previous experiences doesn’t mean that’s the limits of the world.

  • 4

    Find out what makes you happy.Then do that. As much as you want or can.

  • 5

    Make use of your creativity. Take out a piece of paper. Write at the top of the page what area in your life you would like to have more ideas about. Perhaps you want ideas to earn more money or become a healthier person.

  • 6

    Cut down on TV. Do you watch it too much? Watch less of what they are doing in TV-land and do more of what you want to do in life.

  • 7

    Focus on the positive. Learn to think more positively most of the time. Learn to let to go of negative threads of thought before they have a chance to take hold of you.

  • 8

    Compare yourself with yourself. Not with others. Comparing what you have and your results to what other people have and have accomplished can really kill your motivation as it ups your self-doubts.

  • 9

    Remember to have fun. Or create fun in a task. Then you’ll stay motivated to do and finish it.

  • 10

    Act like your heroes. Read about them, watch them, listen to them. Discover what they did that was special and what made them tick.
